How was your day?

action
person

Arey yaaaar! I sometimes wonder how your days go, and wonder if you’d want to know about mine. On most days, I tell you — my life is rather prosaic, but, nevertheless, I’d tell you things if you’d ask. I’d mostly say good. Okay! Maybe I’d say fine. You’d ask me about what I did today, and I’d tell you things, in vague words, that’ll skip your mind, perhaps — it’s prosaic after all. Ah, alright, I’ll just draw it out, or maybe I’ll write about it, I know my way around with things that need a pen, anyhoo.

This isn’t a daily ritual. I’d update it sometimes, from time to time, and time and time again. Every entry with coordinates appears on the map; the rest remain logged, waiting for a location.
